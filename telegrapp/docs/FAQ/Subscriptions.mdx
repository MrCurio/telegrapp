# Payment and Subscriptions Management

:::tip
<GlobalLink>Click here if you want to Install the App Now</GlobalLink>
:::


<details>
  <summary>When do I need to pay and how?</summary>

  **TelegrApp is Free for our Beta users**. When released, payments can be made via marketplace, credit card, PayPal, or other supported methods.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I cancel, deactivate, stop, start, activate, or resume my services and subscription?</summary>

  You can manage your subscription directly from the GHL Marketplace dashboard. To activate your subscription or resume it, simply install it via Marketplace. To uninstall it, you can clik on "Uninstall" in your Marketplace Installed Apps panel. If you encounter issues, feel free to contact support.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How much do you charge for the service?</summary>

  TelegrApp offers flexible pricing plans depending on the features you need. Visit our [Pricing Page](#) for detailed information on the plans available.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Do you have any yearly plans?</summary>

  **Yes.** We offer discounted yearly plans, which save you up to 20% compared to monthly billing. You can switch to an annual plan anytime from your account settings.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What happens if my payment fails?</summary>

  If your payment fails, we will notify you via email and allow a grace period to update your payment information. During this time, your services will remain active. After the grace period, your subscription may be suspended until payment is resolved.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How will I manage all of my locations? Do you provide any admin panel to manage all the locations?</summary>

  **Yes.** TelegrApp includes an admin panel where you can manage all your locations and team members. You can assign permissions, monitor activities, and control access for different accounts from a single interface.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Is it a White Label app? Can I sell it at my price?</summary>

  **Yes.** TelegrApp supports White Labeling, allowing you to rebrand the app and sell it under your name at your chosen price. Contact us for more information on White Label options.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What support is available if I encounter issues?</summary>

  TelegrApp offers 24/7 customer support via live chat and email. Additionally, you can book one-on-one sessions through Zoom or Google Meet for technical assistance.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Are there additional fees for premium features?</summary>

  Some premium features, such as advanced automation tools and bulk messaging, may require an upgrade to a higher plan. Please review our [Features Page](#) for details.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can I try TelegrApp before subscribing?</summary>

  **Yes.** TelegrApp offers a 7-day free trial for all new users. No credit card is required to sign up for the trial.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can I switch between plans?</summary>

  **Yes.** You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account dashboard. Changes will be applied immediately, and any adjustments in pricing will be prorated.
</details>
